WHAT IS A MELLONHEAD?

MellonHeads are doers, artists, programmers, designers, and hackers. A MellonHead is someone who makes cool things and loves sharing their passions with others. MellonHeads is a community of students at Carnegie Mellon University who collaborate on cool projects and enjoy learning from one another.

WHAT DO MELLONHEADS DO?

MellonHeads hosts monthly CollabNights — collaborative micro-hackathons on CMU's campus. CollabNights are a great time for MellonHeads of all majors to come together and collaborate on projects, present what they're working on, and just hang out and talk about how cool others' projects are :-)

GOALS

We bring together students from all disciplines interested in the intersection between education and technology.

Collaboration

Students from different backgrounds and majors come together to work on projects and share their passions for software, hardware, design, and everything in between.

Technology

MellonHeads are constantly experimenting with cutting edge hardware and software, gaining valuable experience with tomorrow's technology.

Outreach

MellonHeads welcomes members of all experience levels and backgrounds. We're here to help you work on awesome projects — whether you're a beginner or super 1337 haxor.
