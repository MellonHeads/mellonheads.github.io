WHAT IS A MELLONHEAD?

A Mellonhead is a doer, a maker, an artist, a programmer, a hacker. A MellonHead is someone who makes cool things. MellonHeads is a community of students at Carnegie Mellon University who collaborate on cool projects and enjoy learning from one another

WHAT DO MELLONHEADS DO?

MellonHeads hosts regular CollabNights which are like mini hackathons on CMU's campus. CollabNights are a great time for MellonHeads to come together and discuss current projects, collaborate on said projects, and just hang out and talk about how cool others' projects are.

GOALS

We bring together students from all disciplines interested in the intersection between education and technology.

Collaboration

Students from different backgrounds and majors come together to work on projects and discuss current news surrounding technology.

Technology

MellonHeads are constantly experimenting with cutting edge hardware and software, gaining valuable experience with tomorrow's technology.

Outreach

MellonHeads welcomes members of all experience levels and backgrounds. We're here to help you make super cool stuff whether you're a bginner or super 1337 haxor.
