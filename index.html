What is a MellonHead?

MellonHeads are doers, artists, programmers, designers, and hackers.

A MellonHead is someone who makes cool things and loves sharing their passions with others.

MellonHeads is a diverse community of students at Carnegie Mellon University who collaborate on cool projects and enjoy learning from one another.
